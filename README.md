# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes 50,000 e-commerce transactions across multiple product categories, customer regions, payment methods, and discount levels to identify revenue drivers and business growth opportunities.

The project combines SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights and executive dashboards.

# 📈 Key Findings

### Revenue Performance

* Revenue is distributed across multiple product categories and regions.
* Certain categories consistently outperform others in total revenue generation.

### Regional Insights

* Regional sales performance varies significantly.
* High-performing regions represent major opportunities for future expansion.

### Discount Analysis

* Higher discounts do not always result in higher revenue.
* Medium discount ranges often generate stronger average order values.

### Customer Satisfaction

* Customer ratings remain relatively stable across categories.
* No major category exhibits severe customer satisfaction issues.

### Payment Behavior

* Digital payment methods dominate customer transactions.
* Customer preference for frictionless payment experiences continues to grow.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Category Optimization

* Increase investment in top-performing categories.
* Expand inventory allocation toward high-revenue products.

### Regional Growth

* Prioritize marketing spend in high-performing regions.
* Investigate opportunities for expansion into underperforming regions.

### Discount Strategy

* Reduce excessive discounting.
* Focus on targeted promotional campaigns instead of blanket discounts.

### Customer Experience

* Improve highly rated customer experiences across all categories.
* Monitor category-specific customer feedback regularly.

### Payment Optimization

* Continue supporting preferred digital payment methods.
* Simplify checkout experiences to reduce purchase friction.
